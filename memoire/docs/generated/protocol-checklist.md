# Protocol Checklist For The Combined Memoire

Source: chapters 4 and 5 of `Protocole_Redaction_EtudesSup.pdf`.

## Structure Required

- Preliminary pages remain mandatory for a `memoire`.
- Essential parts still need:
  - introduction
  - state of the art
  - development
  - conclusion
- In article format, the article is only a subset of the memoire.
- Missing or incomplete scientific parts must be completed in surrounding chapters.
- A linkage explanation must appear before the article(s) to show how the whole document forms one coherent research project.

## Required Avant-propos Metadata For Each Article Chapter

- every author name
- every author affiliation/status
- submission or acceptance date
- current acceptance/publication state
- target or published journal
- accepted article reference
- explicit explanation of how the article contributes to the memoire
- if the article is in English:
  - French title translation
  - French abstract translation
- if the article differs from the submitted/published version:
  - mention of those modifications

## Formatting Constraints

- article layout must be reworked to match the thesis format
- article bibliographies must be removed from the standalone article body
- all references must be merged into the memoire reference list
- bibliography style must be harmonized across the whole document

## Manual Check Before Deposit

- confirm that a single flagship article is acceptable as a combined memoire structure
- replace all placeholders in `source/04-avant-propos.tex`
- confirm final language policy with supervisor if the article stays in English
- check that wrapper chapters fully satisfy chapter 4 requirements
